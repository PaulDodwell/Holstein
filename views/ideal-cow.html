Rotating the 3D Holstein

Hold down left mouse button and move mouse within the frame to rotate the cow in any direction.

On touch devices just touch and move within the frame to rotate.

Zooming in and out

Pinch within the frame to zoom on touch devices.

On PC or Mac, use mouse wheel or right button to zoom.

On Mac touchpad, left-click + 2 fingers to zoom.

On a keyboard, use the arrow keys to zoom.
